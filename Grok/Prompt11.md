**Here’s a list of notable talent/staffing agencies specializing in cloud computing** (including roles like cloud architects, engineers, DevOps/SRE, solutions architects, migrations, AWS/Azure/GCP specialists, infrastructure as code, and hybrid/multi-cloud positions). Cloud talent demand remains very high due to ongoing migrations, modernization, and AI infrastructure needs. These firms handle contract, contract-to-hire, permanent, and staff augmentation placements.

### Major National/Well-Known Agencies with Strong Cloud Practices
- **Insight Global** — Top performer for cloud engineering and infrastructure roles, with fast placements and a large candidate pool for AWS, Azure, and GCP needs.
- **TEKsystems** — Large-scale IT staffing with extensive cloud capabilities, ideal for enterprise migrations, infrastructure, and high-volume contract staffing.
- **Robert Half Technology** — Reliable for cloud roles with AI-powered matching; covers contract to permanent placements across major platforms.
- **KORE1** — Strong in placing pre-vetted cloud engineers (AWS/Azure/GCP) with high retention rates and fast time-to-hire (often ~17 days).
- **Apex Systems** and **Kforce** — Frequently ranked for AI/cloud talent; excellent for scalable infrastructure and DevOps roles.

### Specialized/Boutique Cloud-Focused Agencies
- **Nexus IT Group** — Dedicated cloud recruiters specializing in AWS, Azure, and GCP staffing with a tight focus on technical fit and quick results.
- **Blue Signal Search** — Recognized for managed services and cloud recruiting (architects, engineers, operations) serving startups to enterprises.
- **Focus GTS** (Focus Global Talent Solutions) — Women-owned firm with fast cloud placements (often within 48 hours) in cloud tech, AI, and related areas.
- **Cloud Talent Services** — Boutique firm exclusively focused on cloud and hybrid cloud ecosystems with deep institutional knowledge.
- **Motion Recruitment** — Strong in cloud engineer and infrastructure roles across major tech markets.

### Other Notable Mentions
- **Next Ventures**, **USA Tech Recruit**, **Jefferson Frank** (AWS specialist), **Alpha Apex Group** (data center/cloud infrastructure), and **Scion Technology**.
- Larger players like **Experis** or nearshore options (e.g., **Near**, **Andela**, **GoGloby**) for cost-effective cloud talent augmentation.

**Tips for choosing**:
- For **high-volume/contract/staff augmentation** — Insight Global, TEKsystems, or KORE1 scale effectively.
- For **specialized or senior cloud roles** (architects, migrations, platform engineering) — Boutiques like Nexus IT Group, Blue Signal, or Cloud Talent Services offer deeper expertise in specific platforms and environments.
- Prioritize agencies with strong technical vetting, platform certifications (AWS/Azure/GCP), and experience with IaC tools (Terraform, etc.), Kubernetes, and security/compliance.
- Cloud hiring often overlaps with DevOps, cybersecurity, and data roles — many agencies cover the full stack.
- Evaluate time-to-fill, retention, replacement guarantees, and reviews on Clutch or Glassdoor.
